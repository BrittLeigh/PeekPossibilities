About

Welcome to Peek Possibilities

I graduated with my Master of Occupational Therapy in 2011 from Eastern Washington University. I have worked with adults and children with disabilities as a habilitation aide, service coordinator, PATH therapeutic horseback riding instructor, and occupational therapist. As an occupational therapist I have worked in a skilled nursing facility, the school system, early intervention, and private practice.

I love working with moms and kids! I really enjoy problem-solving to figure out the underlying reason someone is struggling. I have received continuing education and training The Institute for Birth Healing Holistic Treatment of the Pregnant Body, The Institute for Birth Healing Holistic Treatment of the Postpartum Body, The Institute for Birth Healing Advanced Treatment of the Pregnant Body, Herman and Wallace Pelvic Therapy Level 1, pediatric bowel and bladder issues, sensory integration and sensory processing, sensory diets, auditory processing, reflex integration, autism spectrum disorders, neurodevelopmental treatment, pediatric neurodevelopmental disorders, Handwriting Without Tears®, therapeutic pediatric yoga, Myofascial Release, visual processing, Interactive Metronome®, Therapeutic Listening®, and nutrition.

In my free time, I love spending time with my husband, two kids, two dogs, two cats. I enjoy spending time outdoors hiking, camping, and just appreciating our beautiful world.

My vision is every woman feels empowered and supported during pregnancy, birth, and postpartum.

My vision is that every child feels loved, supported and empowered to reach his/her full potential.

My mission is to provide Roswell moms and kids high-quality, individualized therapy services in the natural environment (home/community) focusing on the whole person and family in order to help each person reach their full potential.
